# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of **OpenAuth** pledge to make participation in our community a **harassment-free experience for everyone**, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an **open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community**.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
* Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them
* Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
* Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
* Trolling, deliberate intimidation, or sustained disruption
* Publishing othersâ€™ private information without explicit permission
* Sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take **appropriate and fair corrective action** in response to any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, including:

* GitHub repositories
* Issues and pull requests
* Discussions and comments
* Community communications

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it responsibly.

* Reports should be made **privately**
* All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly
* Reporters will be protected from retaliation

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these **Community Impact Guidelines** in determining consequences:

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact:** Minor violation
**Consequence:** Private, respectful warning with guidance.

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact:** Repeated or more serious violation
**Consequence:** Formal warning outlining consequences for continued behavior.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact:** Sustained or severe violation
**Consequence:** Temporary ban from project interaction.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact:** Extreme or repeated violations
**Consequence:** Permanent removal from the community.
